How to Use yt-dlp-gui:

1. Open the application by running `yt-dlp.py`.

2. Enter the YouTube video or playlist URL in the input box.

3. Choose the format (e.g., MP4, MP3) and quality (e.g., 1080p, 720p, audio only) from the dropdown menus.

4. Click "Select Output Directory" to choose where the downloaded files will be saved.

5. Click "Download" to start downloading the video or playlist.

6. To view your download history, click the "View Download History" button.

Enjoy your downloads!
